# Privacy Policy - CareerLens AI

**Effective Date: January 17, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to CareerLens AI ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your data when you use our web application.

CareerLens AI analyzes YouTube watch history to provide personalized career recommendations through AI-powered insights.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Google Account Information**: When you sign in with Google OAuth, we receive:
  - Your Google account email address
  - Your name and profile picture
  - Basic profile information you authorize

- **YouTube Watch History Data**:
  - Video titles and descriptions
  - Channel information
  - Watch timestamps
  - Video categories and tags

- **Uploaded Files**: If you choose to upload your Google Takeout watch-history.json file

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our application
- **API Usage**: Interactions with Google APIs and AI services

### 2.3 Third-Party Service Data
- **Google APIs**: We use Google OAuth 2.0 and YouTube Data API v3
- **AI Processing**: Google Gemini AI for content analysis
- **Hosting**: Vercel for web hosting and serverless functions

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Career Analysis**: Process your YouTube watch history to identify learning patterns and interests
- **AI Processing**: Use machine learning models to categorize content and generate recommendations
- **Personalization**: Provide tailored career suggestions based on your viewing habits

### 3.2 Technical Operations
- **Authentication**: Verify your identity through Google OAuth
- **Data Processing**: Analyze video metadata and content patterns
- **Service Delivery**: Generate and display career recommendations
- **Security**: Protect against unauthorized access and abuse

### 3.3 Legal Compliance
- **GDPR Compliance**: Honor data subject rights and privacy regulations
- **Data Protection**: Implement security measures to protect your information
- **Transparency**: Maintain clear records of data processing activities

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage Location
- **Frontend Processing**: Most data processing occurs in your browser
- **Server Storage**: Minimal data stored on Vercel servers (encrypted at rest)
- **Session Data**: Temporary storage during analysis sessions

### 5.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest

- **Regular Audits**: Security reviews and vulnerability assessments

### 5.3 Data Retention
- **Analysis Sessions**: Data retained only for the duration of your analysis session
- **Account Data**: Minimal account information retained for service functionality
