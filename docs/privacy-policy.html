Privacy Policy - CareerLens AI

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CareerLens AI ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your data when you use our web application.

CareerLens AI analyzes YouTube watch history to provide personalized career recommendations through AI-powered insights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google OAuth, we receive your Google account email, name, and profile picture
  • YouTube Watch History Data: Video titles, descriptions, channel information, watch timestamps, categories, and tags
  • Uploaded Files: If you choose to upload your Google Takeout watch-history.json file

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our application
  • API Usage: Interactions with Google APIs and AI services

2.3 Third-Party Service Data

We use Google APIs, AI services, and hosting providers that collect their own data according to their privacy policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Career Analysis: Process your YouTube watch history to identify learning patterns and interests
  • AI Processing: Use machine learning models to categorize content and generate recommendations
  • Personalization: Provide tailored career suggestions based on your viewing habits

3.2 Technical Operations

  • Authentication: Verify your identity through Google OAuth
  • Data Processing: Analyze video metadata and content patterns
  • Service Delivery: Generate and display career recommendations
  • Security: Protect against unauthorized access and abuse

3.3 Legal Compliance

We process data to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5.3 Data Retention
